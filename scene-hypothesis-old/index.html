Scene Theory

A New Framework for Understanding Progress and Development

Human excellence is built through scenes - the organic social clusters where innovation thrives. Understanding scene dynamics offers new frameworks for progress and development. Traditional approaches overlook these critical environments where breakthrough work emerges.

This open online book introduces Scene Theory as both an analytical lens and a practical framework. Through original research, including case studies from Nigeria, we explore how scenes function, why they matter for development, and how to create conditions for their formation in challenging environments.
