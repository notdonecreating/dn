Scene Theory: How Social Clusters Drive Progress & Development

By Your Name, 2025

About This Book

An exploration of how scenes—organic social clusters with distinct values and status systems—function as engines of progress, creativity, and development.

This open online book explores how scenes—organic social clusters with distinct values and status systems—function as engines of progress, creativity, and development.

Through a case study of Nigeria and broader theoretical framework, the book offers new perspectives on why some places thrive while others stagnate, and how scene theory can inform more effective development approaches.

How to Use This Book

You can read this book in two ways:

  • Complete Version: Access full chapters with detailed explanations, examples, and analysis by clicking on part or chapter links below.
  • Summary Version: View concise bullet-point summaries of key concepts in the summary version.

  4. Part IV: Breaking Cycles Through Scene Theory

    Offers practical approaches to enabling scene formation in challenging environments and rethinking development interventions.
